Hello, the name is Coleman.

I’m a freelance product designer living in Houston, TX. Inspired by a Denver snowboarder I used to see at the slopes, I started designing my own graphics in 2010 to make my snowboard and skateboard movies more interesting. After a year of doing graphic design, I got bored and started coding websites. A few months of coding passed by and I stumbled upon UI/UX design. I started designing all types of interfaces and it quickly became my passion. I now have over 5 years of experience working full-time as a freelance product designer. Besides design, I’m also passionate about electronic music production, good coffee, and bikes. I constantly strive to keep learning and experiencing the unknown.
